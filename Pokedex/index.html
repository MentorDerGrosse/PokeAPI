PokeApi - PokeDex

This website is a PokeDex for almost 900 different pokemons.
The pokemons are being fetch from this website: https://pokeapi.co/api

There are four differnt sites:

    Index:
    Information-Page: A short infomation of what the website is about - you are reading it rn:)
    Single-Character-Page: A list of all the pokemons displayed in a PokeDex
    Multiple-Character-Page: A list of all pokemons at once displayed as pokemon-cards
    Quiz: Find out what Pokemon you are :D
    To search in the Cards page, just hover over the Pokeball right over the cards!
